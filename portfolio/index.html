New wallet collection

Private collections stay out of Hub cards and all-collections exports. Wallet details edited here stay collection-only, while shared labels may still appear inside it. You can still export this collection by itself.

Export wallet collections

All-collections exports omit private collections. A private collection can still be selected and exported by itself. The file includes its wallet labels plus your shared owner labels and hidden-asset list, and can be imported on another browser. An export containing CULTDAO Funds can also be renamed to dao-funds-presets.json and published beside this site.

CULTDAO Funds snapshot update

A complete JSON backup downloads before the update. Your local wallet names, descriptions, Safe-owner labels, and wallets you added manually will be kept. The preset's curated hidden-asset list will be applied.

Welcome to Portfolio Tracker

A read-only overview for DAO-linked or personal wallet collections.

  • Read-only portfolio. The shared header can remember your wallet session, but this tracker never signs transactions or moves funds.
  • CULTDAO Funds is a historical preset. It remains editable and needs user curation.
  • Personal use is supported. Create separate collections for any wallets you want to follow.
  • Your setup stays in this browser. Export collections to back them up or move them to another device.
